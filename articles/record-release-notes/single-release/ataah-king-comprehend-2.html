Record Release Note

REIGNSOUND™ NEWS: ATAAH KING, SoundOn Global®

Thursday, October 3, 2024

GLOBAL - Reignsound™ Records[1] rapper ATAAH KING² joins forces with TikTok®³ via SoundOn® Global,⁴ TikTok's worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on ATAAH's SoundCloud® record Comprehend⁵.⁶

The Comprehend 2⁷ remix is set for release on October 20, 2024.⁸ Clips from the record will be available through CapCut®⁹ and TikTok's Commercial Music Library™¹⁰ starting October 4th.

To date, Comprehend, currently streaming on Apple Music®¹¹ and still accessible via SoundCloud,⁵ is ATAAH KING's most recent distribution—originally released on December 15th, 2023.¹²

Notably, this date coincided with ATAAH's trial regarding claims of assault on law enforcement, which he asserts are wrongful charges.

To learn more about this incident, visit The Black Wall Street Times™ for their coverage of this troubling example of wrongful conduct within the U.S. justice system.¹³
