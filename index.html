REIGNSOUNDSM PRESS: ATAAH KING, The Black Wall Street TimesTM

Thursday, October 3, 2024

GLOBAL - ReignsoundSM¹ rapper ATAAH KING² joins forces with TikTok®³ via SoundOn® Global,⁴ TikTok's worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on ATAAH's SoundCloud® record Comprehend⁵.⁶

Comprehend (Freestyle)—Remix⁷ is set for release on October 20, 2024.⁸ Clips from the record will be available through CapCut®⁹ and TikTok's Commercial Music LibraryTM¹⁰ starting October 4th.
