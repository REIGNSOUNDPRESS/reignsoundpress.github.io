REIGNSOUND™ NEWS: ATAAH KING, Black Wall Street Times

Thursday, October 3, 2024

GLOBAL - Reignsound™¹ rapper Ataah King² joins forces with TikTok³ via SoundOn Global⁴, TikTok's worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on Ataah's SoundCloud record Comprehend⁵.⁶

Comprehend (Freestyle)—Remix⁷ is set for release on October 20, 2024.⁸ Clips from the record will be available through CapCut⁹ and TikTok's Commercial Music Library¹⁰ starting October 4th.

To date, Comprehend, currently streaming on Apple Music¹¹ and still accessible via SoundCloud⁵, is Ataah King's most recent distribution—originally released on December 15, 2023.¹²

Notably, this date coincided with Ataah's trial regarding claims of assault on law enforcement, which he asserts are wrongful charges.

To learn more about this incident, visit Black Wall Street Times for their coverage of this troubling example of wrongful conduct within the U.S. justice system.¹³
