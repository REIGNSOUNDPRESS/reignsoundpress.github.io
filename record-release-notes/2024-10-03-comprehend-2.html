Record Release Note

REIGNSOUNDSM PRESS: ATAAH KING, The Black Wall Street TimesTM

Thursday, October 3, 2024

GLOBAL - ReignsoundSM1 rapper ATAAH KING2 joins forces with TikTok®3 via SoundOn® Global,4 TikTok's worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on ATAAH's SoundCloud® record Comprehend5.6

Comprehend (Freestyle)—Remix7 is set for release on October 20, 2024.8 Clips from the record will be available through CapCut®9 and TikTok's Commercial Music LibraryTM10 starting October 4th.

To date, Comprehend, currently streaming on Apple Music®11 and still accessible via SoundCloud,5 is ATAAH KING's most recent distribution—originally released on December 15, 2023.12

Notably, this date coincided with ATAAH's trial regarding claims of assault on law enforcement, which he asserts are wrongful charges.

To learn more about this incident, visit The Black Wall Street TimesTM for their coverage of this troubling example of wrongful conduct within the U.S. justice system.13
