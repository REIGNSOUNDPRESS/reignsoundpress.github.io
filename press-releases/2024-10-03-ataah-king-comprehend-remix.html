REIGNSOUND NEWS: Comprehend (Freestyle) Remix by Ataah King

GLOBAL - Reignsound™¹ rapper Ataah King² joins forces with TikTok³ via SoundOn Global⁴, TikTok's worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on Ataah's SoundCloud record Comprehend⁵.⁶

Comprehend (Freestyle)—Remix⁷ is set for release on October 20, 2024.⁸ Clips from the record will be available through CapCut⁹ and TikTok's Commercial Music Library¹⁰ starting October 4th.

To date, Comprehend, currently streaming on Apple Music¹¹, is Ataah King's most recent distribution—originally released on December 15, 2023.¹² Notably, this date coincided with Ataah's trial regarding claims of assault on law enforcement, which he asserts are wrongful charges.

For more information, visit Black Wall Street Times for their coverage of this incident.¹³
