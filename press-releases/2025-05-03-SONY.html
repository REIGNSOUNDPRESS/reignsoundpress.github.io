REIGNSOUNDXLTM: ATAAH KING, GBSTM, IIIAMDESS, JUSS JABZZ, ReignsoundSM Records, Sony® Music

Saturday, May 3, 2025

GLOBAL — Prior to his trial, ATAAH KING, the "NOT ONE"+ and "FAVORITE RAPPER"+ rapper was in talks with Sony® Music, via AWAL® and The Orchard®, regarding the release of his forthcoming album Get Rich or Die Tryin’® 2. The album was intended to follow a promotional campaign titled the “Year-Long Singles Run Project,” in which ATAAH would release a new single every week for a year.

The project was abruptly halted due to legal challenges and ATAAH’s increasingly independent—yet, at the time, deemed "risky"—moves, which, in light of the recent acquisition of the label, prompted Sony® to impose increased restrictions on the rapper's access to the AWAL® music distribution portal—inadvertently, temporarily disrupting the rollout.

Following his release from jail stemming from a wrongful conviction and after an intentionally subdued delivery of his single Comprehend 2 through SoundOn®, ATAAH was picked up by Roc Nation®, securing additional industry support.

Meeting traditional industry benchmarks were not prioritized for this project; however, it still proved to be a record ahead of its time—resonating deeply and setting a new standard for cultural impact that continues to shape current industry trajectory.

This approach allowed ATAAH to build authentic momentum—prioritizing connection over chart metrics—and led to a lasting collaboration with JUSS JABZZ, a key figure in the rollout and an executive for the ReignsoundSM Records label, according to a Business Proposal+ drafted on February 24, 2025.

Their partnership remains central to the label’s creative direction and long-term vision, now backed by STRM® Music, a cutting-edge São Paulo-based record label in Brazil.

The current aim is to reestablish alignment with Sony® Music and ATAAH is still actively exploring collaborative opportunities with music distribution teams and companies—including Sony's United Kingdom-based subsidiary, Robots + HumansTM—as part of a diversified distribution and development strategy, alongside Universal Music Group's Motown® Records.

An internal memo shared with ReignsoundSM labelmate IIIAMDESS confirms that a formal reach-out is scheduled for this upcoming week.

This renewed effort seeks to rebuild ties and gain the structural backing necessary for upcoming releases—especially as ATAAH’s presence grows globally.

ReignsoundSM Records, currently led by JUSS JABZZ, is also drafting contracts to bring key members back on board. Additionally, Grillz by ScottyTM (GBS) and other culturally relevant brands—such as the LIVE on Edgewood and Indie on Edgewood series—are strategic pillars being integrated to support broader growth initiatives.

For the RECORD●:
ReignsoundSM recognizes the current absence of expanded internal coverage surrounding breakout artist IIIAMDESS, which is not due to "undersight," but rather a tactical decision as the label ensures both flagship artists—ATAAH KING and IIIAMDESS—are positioned solidly before ramping up additional campaigns.

During a recent Twitch® LIVE stream titled "Cook w Khyra,"+ hosted by IIIAMDESS; IIIAMDESS gave viewers a snapshot of where she's at creatively and personally.

Without taking away from the significance of ATAAH KING’s ongoing month-long detox, IIIAMDESS’s DESSATHONTM series offers a complementary and harmonious counterbalance as ATAAH prepares to resume working on music full-time.

It’s worth noting that ATAAH has not publicly indicated any plans to part ways with weed entirely, but is currently on a personal break as he prepares to present his legal situation to organizations including the ACLU®, NAACP®, Southern Poverty Law Center, and Lawyers' Committee for Civil Rights Under Law—with the goal of having them re-present his case to the United States' Department of Justice.

Recently, IIIAMDESS led a successful DESSATHONTM in New York City, collaborating with several other well-known influencers and entertainers, and buzz is already building around the Texas DESSATHONTM—with further details expected to be shared soon.

Expanded press and editorial coverage is on the way as well, to shed more light on IIIAMDESS’s evolution—from her earlier days with INFLUENCER CITY's Bratz HouseTM+ to her growing influence as the flagship artist under the ReignsoundSM Records imprint.
