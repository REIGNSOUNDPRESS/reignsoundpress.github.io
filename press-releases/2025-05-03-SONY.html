REIGNSOUNDXLTM: ATAAH KING, GBSTM, IIIAMDE$$, JUSS JABZZ, ReignsoundSM Records — Sony® Music

Saturday, May 3, 2025

GLOBAL — Prior to his trial, ATAAH KING, the NOT ONESM+ and FAVORITE RAPPERSM+ rapper, was in talks with Sony® Music, via AWAL® and The Orchard®, regarding the release of his forthcoming project Get Rich or Die Tryin’® 2.*

The album was intended to follow a promotional campaign titled the Year-Long Singles Run Project,* in which ATAAH would release a new single every week for a year.*

The project was abruptly halted due to legal challenges and ATAAH’s increasingly independent—yet, at the time, deemed "risky"—moves, which, in light of the recent acquisition of the label, prompted Sony® to impose increased restrictions on the rapper's access to the AWAL® music distribution portal—inadvertently, temporarily disrupting the rollout.*

Following his release, stemming from a wrongful conviction, and after an intentionally subdued delivery of his single Comprehend 2 through SoundOn®, ATAAH was picked up by Roc Nation®, securing industry support through the provision of a guaranteed outlet to continue distributing music.[1]

Meeting traditional market benchmarks were not prioritized for this project; however, it still proved to be a record ahead of its time—resonating deeply and setting a new standard for cultural impact that continues to shape current industry trajectory.[2]

This approach allowed ATAAH to build authentic momentum—prioritizing connection over chart metrics—and led to a lasting collaboration with JUSS JABZZ+, a key figure in the rollout and an executive for the ReignsoundSM Records label, according to a drafted Business Proposal.[3]

This partnership remains central to the label’s creative direction and long-term vision, now backed by STRM® Music+, a cutting-edge São Paulo-based record label in Brazil.[1]

The current aim is to reestablish alignment with Sony® Music, and ReignsoundSM is still actively exploring collaborative opportunities with music distribution teams and companies—including Sony's United Kingdom-based subsidiary, Robots + HumansTM+—as part of a diversified distribution and development strategy, alongside Universal Music Group's Motown® Records+.*

An internal memo shared with ReignsoundSM labelmate IIIAMDESS+ confirms that a formal reach-out is scheduled for the upcoming week.*

This renewed effort seeks to rebuild ties and gain the structural backing necessary for upcoming releases—as Reignsound’s presence grows globally.*

ReignsoundSM Records, currently led by JUSS JABZZ, is also in the process of bringing key members back on board. Grillz by ScottyTM (GBS)+, operated by Atlanta rapper and businessman Scotty ATL and Micah Kiyo, performing artist and FEMININE ENERGY 101 CEO, and other culturally relevant brands—including the LIVE on Edgewood+ and Indie on Edgewood+ series—are strategic pillars integrated to support broader growth initiatives.*
