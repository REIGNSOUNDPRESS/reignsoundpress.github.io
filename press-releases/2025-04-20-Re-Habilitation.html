REIGNSOUNDSM PRESS: ATAAH KING, ReignsoundSM Re:Habilitation

Sunday, April 20, 2025

GLOBAL - ATAAH KING, ReignsoundSM Records rapper known for his polarizing honesty and cryptic lyricism, has just made a statement that has both supporters and insiders paying attention:

"I'ma go a month without lightin' one." ― ATAAH KING (via memo to REIGNSOUNDXLTM)

Delivered exclusively through REIGNSOUNDXLTM, the announcement isn’t just about taking a break from weed—but also indicative of a spiritual pivot, personal experiment, and perhaps even a cultural reset.[1]

This new phase builds on an earlier effort from the top of the year, when ATAAH participated in a Re:Habilitation pilot project by ReignsoundSM leaning on community support to begin detoxing from regular consumption of marijuana.[2] While that attempt planted seeds, this time ATAAH's taking a slightly different route—one that is more internally driven.

With professional psychologists by his side and a deeper reliance on faith and God-given willpower, the rapper seems committed to redefining what recovery looks like in real-time.[3]

All the while, behind the scenes a larger story is unfolding: lawsuits citing civil rights violations and the formal launch of the ReignsoundSM brand enterprise, including the official establishment of ReignsoundSM Records.[4]

These aren’t just business moves, but also personal turning points with each thread weaving into the next, and many details still forthcoming.

As of right now, we know: Grillz By ScottyTM+, the Atlanta-based global jewelry brand, has played a relatively quiet but strong and consistent role in this process; names like JUSS JABZZ+ and IIIAMDESS+ have been spotted nearby without much clarification on the extent of the relationships; there's also been movement around and including members of what’s being referred to as the “Reignsound Group Family;”[5] as well as involvement with and affiliation to ROC Nation®.[4][6][7]
